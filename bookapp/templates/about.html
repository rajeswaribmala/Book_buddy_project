About BookBuddy 📚

BookBuddy is your personalized online bookshelf, designed to help you store, organize, and share your favorite books in one secure place.

You can easily add your books with descriptions, authors, and PDF uploads. Other users can view and download your books, but only you can edit or delete them.

Whether you're a student, a writer, or a book lover — BookBuddy lets you maintain your digital library from anywhere.

Join us, start uploading your collection, and discover books from fellow readers too!
